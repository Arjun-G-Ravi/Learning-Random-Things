Facts about bears

Bears are the largest land carnivores in North America. They can weigh up to 1,000 pounds and have a wingspan of more than 20 feet. Bears are omnivores, meaning they eat a wide variety of plants and animals, including deer, elk, moose, caribou, bighorn sheep, musk oxen, antelope, and bison.
